Healthcare

Never before have healthcare providers faced such a daunting combination of regulatory, market and financial challenges. With a team of more than 55 experienced, industry-focused lawyers, McGuireWoods helps clients address ongoing, day-to-day obstacles and create new models for delivering healthcare services in the United States and abroad.

McGuireWoods’ healthcare group and individual lawyers from across the country are ranked by Chambers USA, Best Lawyers in America, Legal Elite and Super Lawyers as among the top legal providers in the nation. Clients recognize the value of the team’s depth in regulatory, litigation and transactional matters, noting our “great commercial awareness,” and describe us as “very skilled,” “extremely knowledgeable, practical and accessible.” We are sought out for opinions and insights on healthcare issues by industry journals and major news organizations ranging from The Daily Law Bulletin and American Health Lawyers, to Medical Laboratory Observer, MSNBC and HFM (a publication of the Healthcare Financial Management Association).

Clients come to McGuireWoods from all sectors of the healthcare industry. We advise academic medical centers, ambulatory surgery centers, assisted-living and long-term care facilities, dialysis centers, hospitals and hospital systems, hospices, imaging facilities, clinical laboratories, nursing homes, management service organizations, physician-hospital organizations, pharmacies and specialty medical clinics, as well as medical staff organizations and medical, healthcare and related trade associations. We also represent venture capital firms, commercial banks, specialty lenders, investment bankers and individual investors in healthcare-related transactions.

McGuireWoods’ representation of healthcare industry clients spans all major areas of law. Our healthcare transaction lawyers advise clients on corporate organization and governance, investments by third parties, and debt finance and securities transactions. The healthcare regulation and policy team counsels on compliance, U.S. Food and Drug Administration requirements, intellectual property, information technology and privacy concerns (including HIPAA and the HITECH Act), reimbursement, certificates of need and developments relating to healthcare reform. When disputes, investigations and law enforcement actions arise, the healthcare litigation lawyers provide critical, intelligent defense of companies, executives, boards of directors and other key individuals in federal and state courts across the country.

In addition, the firm guides healthcare businesses on litigation, antitrust, government investigations, securities, labor and employment, financing, employee benefits, real estate and other matters.

Since healthcare client matters often extend beyond traditionally defined business and legal disciplines, McGuireWoods assembles teams of lawyers from the firm’s other key practice areas to develop and deliver targeted solutions and advice. The firm also draws on the resources of its full-service public affairs subsidiary, McGuireWoods Consulting, to provide strategic communications and government relations services.

Ranked nationwide for cannabis law and for healthcare, and recognized statewide for healthcare in California, District of Columbia, Illinois.

Description
Today's healthcare, pharmaceutical, life sciences and medical devices enterprises face a daunting array of legal and business issues, many of which exist in no other sector of the global economy. Among these unique challenges are regulatory compliance, credentialing, fraud and abuse prevention, product liability and payment issues. These concerns arise on top of the numerous day-to-day and strategic issues that confront every company, whether domestic or multinational, publicly traded or privately held.
